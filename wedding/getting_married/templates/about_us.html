About us

Kris and Marissa met in 2005 while working at Lowe's Foods in Charlotte, NC. They were great friends while working together. They lost touch after Kris got a different job and Marissa moved to Raleigh, NC. After losing contact for several years, Kris decided to contact Marissa through Facebook. A few Facebook conversations, turned into a few text messages which turned into Kris driving to Raleigh from Indian Land, SC. June 15, 2011, changed both of their lives forever!
After dating for almost two years, Kris proposed to Marissa on May 25, 2013, in North Myrtle Beach, SC. They were going to spend some time down at the beach for Memorial Day Weekend with some friends. Kris took Marissa to one of her favorite resturants down at the beach, California Dreaming. After an early dinner, Kris decided he wanted to take some pictures on the beach. Even though this was out of the norm for Kris, Marissa though it was a great idea because it was such a beautiful evening. After a few pictures, Kris got down on one knee to ask Marissa to marry him, and of course she said 'yes'!
